Lasagna

Description

This yummy lasagna is sure to whet your appetite! Made from only the finest ingredients, we worked hard to ensure that this lasagna is not only the tastiest you've ever had, but healthy too! (Health claims not verified by the FDA)

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • That crumbly cheese that doesn't melt
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Ground Pork

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Mix tomato sauce, tomato paste, and ground pork together
  3. Line the bottom of the pan with lasagna pasta
  4. Pour sauce mixture over pasta
  5. Layer both cheeses over sauce layer
  6. Repeat process of layering pasta, sauce, and cheese until you have reached the desired height
  7. Bake for 1 hour, or until golden brown on top
  8. Let cool, enjoy!
