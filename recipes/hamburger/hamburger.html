Hamburger

Description

This delicious hamburger is sure to be a crowd pleaser at your next BBQ or event! It's simple to make, uses only the best ingredients and will leave you wanting more!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun
  • Ground Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to high heat
  2. Form ground beef into a ball, and then lightly press it so it forms a flat circle
  3. Place patty on grill, cooking 4 minutes per side, and onl flipping once
  4. Cut hamburger bun in half
  5. Place cooked patty on bun, top with desired toppings
  6. Serve!
