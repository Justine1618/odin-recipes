Easy Midweek Pizza

Details

This pizza is so easy, you'll wonder why you ever make anything else! All you need are 4 simple ingredients, an oven, and a sense of adventure!

Ingredients

  • Frozen Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzerella Chees
  • Pepperoni or topping of choice

Steps

  1. The night before, allow your pizza dough to defrost in the fridge.
  2. Preheat your oven as high as it will go.
  3. On a baking sheet, strech your pizza dough into a 12" circle
  4. Spread your tomato sauce onto the pizza, leaving about half and inch around the edges for the crust.
  5. Spread your grated cheese over the sauce.
  6. Top with your toppings of choice.
  7. Place in your oven, and allow to bake until the cheese is melted, bubling, and brown.
  8. Slice into 8 slices, and serve!
